Origin Story

After training for many years as a culinary student and working for many top tier chefs, I decied to take a 1 month vacation to Thailand. The food and culture changed my life. Paris didn't have food even close to what I experinced on my travels. I knew that I needed to show the people of Paris what true Thai food was all about!
